Cow Care Basics

Healthy cattle require proper nutrition, clean water, regular health care, and safe handling practices. Good management leads to healthier animals and more successful ranching.

Nutrition

Provide quality forage, fresh water, and balanced minerals to keep cattle healthy.

Calving

Monitor pregnant cows and provide a clean, safe environment during calving season.

Health & Vaccinations

Routine vaccinations and health checks help prevent disease and improve herd health.

Handling & Safety

Move cattle calmly and use proper facilities to reduce stress and prevent injuries.

Pasture Management

Rotate grazing areas and maintain healthy grass to provide consistent nutrition for your herd.

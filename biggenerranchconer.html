Beginner Rancher Corner

Starting your first herd can be exciting and challenging. These beginner tips will help you make informed decisions and build confidence as a new rancher.

What to Look for in Your First Cow

Choose healthy cattle with calm temperaments, bright eyes, good body condition, and no signs of illness or injury.

How Many Acres Per Cow?

The amount of land needed depends on your climate, pasture quality, and available forage.

Best Breeds for Beginners

Breeds with calm personalities and easy management are excellent choices for first-time ranchers.

Common Mistakes New Ranchers Make

Avoid overcrowding pastures, skipping vaccinations, and purchasing cattle without researching the breed.

Understanding Cattle Body Language

Watching ear position, movement, posture, and vocalizations helps you recognize stress, comfort, or illness.
